A2 Geeks

A2Geeks is a nonprofit dedicated to making southeast Michigan and Ann Arbor in particular, a great place for geeks and creatives to live, work, and play. We support individuals and groups seeking to build community around issues or projects they are passionate about and to educate others in the area.

A dash of electronics, a pinch of art, a scoop of biology and a MONSTER amount of awesome! All Hands Active is a 501(c)(3) non-profit community organization. Ann Arbor Design Group

Ann Arbor Design Group is a collection of interdisciplinary designers (UI/UX, graphic, product, architecture, etc). We host speakers, show-and-tells, design jams, and other events to help designers and design students in Ann Arbor connect, learn, find work, and hire design talent.
